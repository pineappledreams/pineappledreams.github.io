Menu Tag Creating Excel macro for Banquets

An Excel macro that saved 1 person's entire workday

While I was working in Hilton Tokyo and finally had some time to look around a bit,
I noticed how the menu tags were made. There was one waitress whose routine work was to
copy paste the tag text into the right place one by one.
It took about fifteen to thirty minutes to finish one buffet worth of tags, and we had more than 8 buffets per week.
To save time and money for our team, I made an Excel macro that would do the same amount of work in 2 minutes.
